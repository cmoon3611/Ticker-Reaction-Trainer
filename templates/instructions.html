Ticker Reaction Trainer

The Trainer mode helps you practice quickly identifying stock tickers mentioned in headlines and reacting with the correct trade classification.

  • Headline Display: Each headline is shown at the top in uppercase letters.
  • Ticker Input: Enter the ticker symbol you think is relevant to the headline, then press Enter.
  • Trade Input: After a correct ticker input, enter the trade classification by pressing one of the keys with Shift:
    • Shift + F = Large Long (A)
    • Shift + D = Small Long (B)
    • Shift + K = Small Short (C)
    • Shift + J = Large Short (D)
  • Feedback: You'll see immediate feedback indicating if your ticker or trade input was correct or incorrect.
  • Timer: The timer counts how long you take per headline, helping you improve your speed.

Burst Mode - 10 Headline Test

Burst Mode is a timed challenge where you respond to 10 headlines in a row as fast and accurately as possible.

  • For each headline, enter the tickers and corresponding trades as you would in Trainer mode.
  • The timer runs continuously per headline and only stops after all tickers in that headline are graded.
  • After 10 headlines, you will see detailed statistics including max, mode, median, average, and standard deviation of your reaction times, plus accuracy.
  • You can review your answers in a table and restart the test anytime with the provided button.

Adding and Editing Headlines

You can add or edit headlines through the "Edit Headlines" page:

  • Add new headlines along with their relevant tickers and categories.
  • Edit existing headlines or remove outdated ones.
  • This helps keep your training material fresh and customized to your needs.

Controls Summary

  • Ticker Input: Type ticker and press Enter.
  • Trade Input: Use Shift + F/D/K/J keys to grade trades quickly.
  • Restart Burst Mode: Click the button on the results popup to retry the test.
  • Navigation: Use the Home button in the header to return to the main page at any time.

Practice regularly to improve your speed and accuracy in recognizing market-moving headlines and making quick trading decisions!
